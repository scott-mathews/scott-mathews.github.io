About Me

I graduated from Indiana University Bloomington with both a B.S. and M.S. in Computer Science in 2018, and joined Microsoft full time as a Software Engineer. From building voice-enabled services for Cortana, to integrating advances in natural language processing into Outlook's search capabilities, I have a breadth of experience creating and maintaining intelligence-based features for large audiences.
